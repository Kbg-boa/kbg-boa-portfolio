## 🎯 3 SIMPLE STEPS TO LAUNCH

### STEP 1: Deploy to Vercel (5 minutes)

1. **Go to:** https://vercel.com
2. **Sign up** with email or GitHub
3. **Click:** "Add New Project"
4. **Choose:** "Deploy without Git" OR drag & drop your folder
5. **Wait:** 2-3 minutes for build
6. **Done!** You get a live URL: `your-project.vercel.app`

---

### STEP 2: Configure Security (3 minutes)

1. **Go to:** https://dash.cloudflare.com
2. **Sign up** (free account)
3. **Click:** "Turnstile" in sidebar
4. **Add Site:**
   - Name: KBG BOA Portfolio
   - Domain: `your-project.vercel.app` (from Step 1)
   - Widget: Managed
5. **Copy** the Site Key (starts with 0x...)
6. **Update** file: `/src/app/components/Contact.tsx` line 259:
   ```javascript
   siteKey="YOUR_PRODUCTION_KEY_HERE"
   ```
7. **Redeploy** on Vercel (drag folder again)

---

### STEP 3: Test Everything (2 minutes)

Visit your live site and test:

- ✅ Click "Email Me" button → Opens email?
- ✅ Fill contact form → Turnstile appears?
- ✅ Submit without Turnstile → Shows error?
- ✅ Complete Turnstile → Can submit?
- ✅ WhatsApp buttons → Open WhatsApp?
- ✅ Test on phone → Everything works?
